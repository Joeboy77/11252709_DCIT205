Explore

The Department of Computer Science has gained a stellar reputation for excellence, which is demonstrated by its highly esteemed staff, state-of-the-art research skills, and ample resources from a top university.

In every facet of computer science—operating systems, compilers, databases, networking, distributed systems, parallel systems, image-based modeling and rendering, intelligent systems for problem-solving and education, computer graphics, and theoretical computer science—faculty and students work closely across disciplines to translate bold new ideas into ground-breaking outcomes. The dynamic curriculum combines Computer Science and Information Technology, two very different fields.

Undergraduate Programs

The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

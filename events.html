Past Events

Becoming a World Class Software Engineer

Becoming a World Class Software Engineer is an event that aims to provide valuable insights and guidance to aspiring and experienced software engineers who are looking to enhance their skills and excel in their careers. The event will bring together industry experts, thought leaders, and successful professionals to share their knowledge and experiences. Participants will have the opportunity to network with like-minded professionals, connect with potential mentors, and gain valuable insights into the industry. The event aims to inspire and motivate attendees to strive for excellence and become world-class software engineers.

Global CyberTech Workshop

The Global CyberTech Workshop is an event focused on cybersecurity and technology. It aims to bring together experts, researchers, industry professionals, and enthusiasts from around the world to discuss and explore the latest advancements, challenges, and trends in the field of cybersecurity By attending the Global CyberTech Workshop, individuals will have the chance to enhance their knowledge, expand their professional network, and stay updated on the latest developments in cybersecurity. Whether you are a cybersecurity professional, a researcher, or simply interested in the field, this event offers a valuable opportunity to deepen your understanding and contribute to the global cybersecurity community.

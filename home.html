About the Department

Transforming our world through technologies

Computers and computational thinking are changing everything. At University of Ghana's Department of Computer Science, we aim to lead that change.

University of Ghana's Computer Science Department is an ideal hub for highly interdisciplinary computer science education and research. With numerous collaborations across disciplines at Africa's top-ranked schools, students and faculty have the power to explode the possibilities of computer science. The department, which has radically expanded its faculty and moved to state-of-the-art facilities to support its vision, also fosters a culture of inclusion, mentorship, and student support. This combination offers our students and researchers an experience like no other. Read More
